Main Page "CREATE"

Close up look of the page

This is the main page of the app. It lets you create your own portfolio based on templates given. You are able to drag your own pictures by just clicking on the box. This automatically makes a mask for your picture with the given box size. From the corners of the box you are able to resize the template box and position them the way you want it. To change the text you just have to click on it. You are able to change the font of the text as well.
